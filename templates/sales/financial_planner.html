STEP 2  ·  Corpus Required at Retirement
🏦
Corpus Needed
—
Growing annuity — expenses inflate every year in retirement
Corpus funds 25 years of inflation-growing withdrawals.
Formula: PMT × [1 − ((1+g)/(1+r))ⁿ] / (r−g)
where PMT = 1st yr expense, g = inflation, r = return, n = retirement years.
STEP 3  ·  Monthly SIP to Achieve Corpus (PMT Formula)
📈
Monthly SIP Required
—
Invest monthly at expected return for years to retirement
Uses expected return (12%) — the pre-retirement accumulation rate.
Formula: PMT = Corpus / [((1+r/12)^n − 1) / (r/12)]
where n = months to retirement.
